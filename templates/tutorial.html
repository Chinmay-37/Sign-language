How to Use Sign Language Conversion

Follow these steps to convert sign language into text effortlessly.

1. Image Acquisition from Camera

Ensure your camera is on and positioned correctly to capture your hand gestures.

2. Hand Detection and Tracing

The system detects your hand and tracks its movements in real-time.

3. Hand Region Segmentation

Your hand is isolated from the background for better recognition.

4. Hand Posture Recognition

The system identifies the posture and maps it to a corresponding text or action.

5. Output on Screen

The recognized sign is converted into text and displayed on the screen.
